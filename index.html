08 — Questions

Straight
Answers

No runaround. No vague promises. If we're a fit, you'll know it in the first call.

What exactly do you do?expand_more
We manage your paid search campaigns on Google and Local Services Ads, build or optimize the landing pages your leads land on, track and manage every inquiry, and maintain your Google Business profile. Each month we review performance, make improvements, and send you a clear report showing what your marketing is producing.
Do I have to sign a long contract?expand_more
We require a 3-month minimum to allow campaigns time to build momentum and optimize. After that, it's month-to-month. We don't lock clients in — we retain them by producing value, not by contracts.
How will I know if it's working?expand_more
Every month you'll get a simple, plain-English report showing: total calls and leads, which turned into booked jobs, and the cost per job. No confusing dashboards — just the numbers that matter to your business.
What does it cost?expand_more
Our management fee depends on scope and market, typically $3,000–$10,000 per month. Ad spend goes directly to the platforms and is separate from our fee. We'll give you an exact quote after our first call.
How soon will I start getting calls?expand_more
Once your campaigns and tracking are live, you'll start seeing inquiries come in. It typically takes 60–90 days to fully optimize targeting and see more predictable results. We set expectations upfront so you know what the timeline looks like.
