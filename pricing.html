The retainer fees above cover strategy, campaign management, creative, funnel builds, automation, reporting, and all system operations. They do not include your advertising budget — the money that goes directly to Google and Meta to run your ads.

We'll recommend a media spend level in your Growth Plan session based on your market, your revenue goals, and the competitive landscape. Most clients at the Growth Engine tier invest $1,500–$4,000/month in media spend alongside the retainer.

The total investment — retainer plus media — is what generates the pipeline. We make sure every dollar is accountable.
